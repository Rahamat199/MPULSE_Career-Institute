Karnataka CET

Our specialized CET program focuses on the specific pattern and syllabus of the Karnataka Common Entrance Test, with additional emphasis on Mathematics for engineering aspirants.

Key Features:
  • Complete coverage of CET syllabus (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology)
  • Special focus on Karnataka PUC syllabus alignment
  • Previous year question paper analysis and pattern recognition
  • Time management strategies for CET exam
  • Regular mock tests with CET-specific marking scheme

MEET (Manipal)

Our MEET program is tailored to help students crack the Manipal Engineering Entrance Test with specialized coaching in all subjects and test-taking strategies.

Key Features:
  • Complete MEET syllabus coverage
  • Focus on application-based questions
  • Special workshops for English and General Aptitude sections
  • Regular full-length MEET mock tests
  • Performance analysis and improvement plans
